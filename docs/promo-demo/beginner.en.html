the learning loop

Diagnose manually, ask AI with the same evidence, then open the answer.

1. Manual firstInspect the failed gate, serial output, and manifest. Record symptoms, hypotheses, ruled-out causes, and time spent.
2. AI secondGive AI the same evidence and require reasoning from logs, gate results, and raw values only.
3. Answer lastOpen the answer key only after comparison; it validates the quality of the diagnosis.
