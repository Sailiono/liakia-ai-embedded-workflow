control model

AI is the throttle. The engineer is the steering wheel and brake.

This is not a demo of AI writing firmware by itself. It shows how an engineer connects AI to a real hardware loop: the engineer provides direction, judgement, and risk boundaries while AI accelerates execution, fills gaps, organizes evidence, and captures proven routines as reusable skills.
AI accelerates execution

Throttle

  • Runs build / flash / test steps quickly inside a defined process.
  • Reads logs, summaries, and manifests to catch missing checks.
  • Drafts diagnosis packets, reports, and next-step proposals.
  • Captures proven workflows as skills for repeated use.
Engineer controls direction

Steering and brake

  • Defines objectives, risk boundaries, and what PASS means.
  • Decides which failures become long-term regression gates.
  • Controls flash, reset, and register-read hardware boundaries.
  • Reviews whether evidence is enough for acceptance and handoff.
The engineer defines the method; AI executes it. The engineer captures the workflow; AI reuses it. Speed comes from AI. Trust comes from engineering boundaries and evidence loops.
